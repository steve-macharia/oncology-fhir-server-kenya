Resource Profile: Oncology Kenya Location

Official URL: http://example.org/StructureDefinition/oncology-kenya-location Version: 0.1.0
Draft as of 2025-06-30 Computable Name: OncologyKenyaLocation

FHIR Location profile tailored for cancer care delivery points in Kenya. Supports capture of facilities relevant to cancer diagnosis, treatment, and palliative care, aligned with national cancer control strategies and KEMRI cancer registry reporting.

Usages:

  • Examples for this Profile: Kenyatta National Hospital - Cancer Treatment Centre and Nyandarua County Referral Hospital - Oncology Unit
