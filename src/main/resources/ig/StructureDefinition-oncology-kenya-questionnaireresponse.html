Resource Profile: Oncology Questionnaire Response Kenya

Official URL: http://example.org/StructureDefinition/oncology-kenya-questionnaireresponse Version: 0.1.0
Draft as of 2025-06-30 Computable Name: OncologyKenyaQuestionnaireResponse

Structured response to oncology-related questionnaires used in Kenyan cancer screening, diagnosis, treatment, palliative care, and national registry reporting workflows. Aligns with Kenya’s National Cancer Control Strategy (2023–2027), Cancer Policy (2020–2030), KEMRI data elements, and county registry efforts.

Usages:

  • Examples for this Profile: QuestionnaireResponse/example-oncology-questionnaireresponse
